About Me

Hello! I am Leen Aboukhalil, and I am a dedicated computer programming and analysis student. After high school, I initially pursued a diploma in network administration, intending to specialize in cybersecurity. Unfortunately, the cybersecurity program was full, so I enrolled in computer programming and analysis for one semester, planning to transfer to cybersecurity afterward.

However, during that first semester, I discovered my true passion for programming. The challenges and excitement of coding deeply motivated me, solidifying my commitment to a lifelong career in programming. I realized that this was where I truly belonged, and I chose to continue pursuing programming instead of transferring.

I am eager to continue learning and growing in this field every day.
